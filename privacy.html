Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

1. No Personal Data Collection

The Red Flag Registry does not collect, store, or process any personal data. We do not ask for names, email addresses, or identifying information to use the platform.

2. Public Information Use Only

All data indexed or displayed by The Red Flag Registry is publicly available and originally published by the user or platform from which it was obtained. We do not access, use, or store any private or restricted content.

3. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use cookies, web beacons, third-party trackers, or analytics tools. We do not track or record IP addresses, browsing behavior, or user search queries.

4. Search Function Privacy

Search queries (such as names or usernames) entered into The Red Flag Registry are processed anonymously. No history or logs of searches are retained.

5. Feedback and Contact

If you choose to contact us via the provided contact form, you are not required to submit any personal information. Messages are submitted anonymously unless you voluntarily provide identifying details.

6. Legal Jurisdiction

This platform operates under the jurisdiction of the United States. Should a legal entity be formed, this privacy policy may be updated to reflect applicable laws in that jurisdiction.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Updates will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically.

Contact: For any privacy-related inquiries, please use the anonymous contact form available on the site.
